Welcome to WADco Media

We create entertaining podcasts for pop culture lovers of all ages. Check out our patreon for exclusive behind the scenes content!

the shows

The WADcast

Join Brandon and Josh every Friday night as they talk about writers, actors, directors, and every job in between! They also touch on hot button issues in the entertainment world and discuss the good and the bad of Hollywood.

The Movie Potluck

Pull up a seat! Jen and her guests gather around the table and discuss movies centered around a theme. Afterwards they offer food and drink recipes based on the featured films.

the hosts

Josh

Joshua Chitty is a writer and actor currently residing in Jacksonville, Florida. He enjoys playing crappy video games and watching movies of varying levels of quality.

Brandon

Brandon is a writer/director currently residing in Long Beach, California. He is one of the co-hosts for the WADcast and spends the rest of his time adoring the love of his life... Indiana Jones.

Jen

Jen currently lives in Hawaii and is the host of WADco Media's The Movie Potluck. Jen also runs the MoviesandMeals website and is also a contributor to the WADcast.

Kinte

Kinte is a radio host/film maker who currently resides in Los Angeles, California. He is a frequent contributor to the Movie Potluck and The WADcast.

Tory

Tory is a podcaster currently residing in Parts Unknown. She hosts the Infectious Geek and is a frequent contributor to the Movie Potluck.

Betty

Betty is currently residing in...the United States? Maybe? She is a frequent contributor to the Movie Potluck.
